Welcome — Next Steps

You've been accepted to proceed. The goal of this intake is simple: so our first consult is focused on insight and decisions — not paperwork.

How It Works
  • Module A: Context (~5 minutes) Who you are, what matters, who's on your care team
  • Module B: Safety & Medical Background (~8-10 minutes) Key history, medications, and safety checks
  • Module C: Priorities (~7-10 minutes) Goals, constraints, readiness, and what success looks like

You can complete this in one sitting or across a day or two. Your progress is automatically saved.

Why We Do It This Way

Most healthcare spends your paid time collecting basic information. We do the opposite: we collect it upfront so we can use our time together for interpretation, prioritization, and a plan that fits your real life.

Important Boundaries

This intake does not provide diagnosis or treatment. This process collects information to prepare for your consultation. It is not an emergency service and does not replace your GP or existing specialist care.

If you have urgent symptoms (chest pain, severe breathlessness, suicidal thoughts, or anything you feel is an emergency), contact emergency services or your usual doctor immediately.

This service is designed to support your health journey and works alongside — not instead of — your existing medical care.

Privacy

We treat your information as confidential and limit access to the team supporting your care. We don't sell your data.

If you'd like a copy of what you submitted or want it deleted, contact us at contact@primeperformancehealth.com.au
